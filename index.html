Hi! I am Syeda Farogh Zahra

I’m a Unity Game Developer with 2+ years of experience crafting immersive gameplay experiences and scalable UI systems. I’ve shipped three commercial titles across Android/iOS, WebGL, and Telegram, including a large-scale 4X multiplayer strategy game.

My primary tools are Unity3D and C#, and I specialize in developing responsive UIs, gameplay systems, and optimized mechanics for both 2D and 3D games. I follow clean architecture principles and value performance, maintainability, and collaboration in every project.

In addition to game development, I have a strong academic foundation in Computer Science from PUCIT. I’ve built projects involving AI/ML models using Python (Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch) and full-stack web applications using Java, JSP, Servlets, and C#. I also have hands-on experience in database design and mobile development.

My goal is to continue building games that are not only technically solid but also meaningful and engaging for players. Feel free to explore my work and get in touch!
